How to Use Easy Worship Trainer

  1. Select number of scriptures or time:
    Use the selection pane to choose how many verses you’ll attempt or seconds to count down from.
  2. Focus an input box:
    Click on Book, Chapter, or Verse field. When you press any key, the timer starts and you begin typing the displayed scripture.
  3. Navigate between fields:
    You can move focus using the same keys as EasyWorship: Arrow Keys, Space Bar, Tab, etc.
  4. Submit each verse:
    Press Enter when you’ve typed the verse exactly as shown. A new scripture will then appear.
  5. Finish & see results:
    After completing your selected count or when the countdown ends, your results will be displayed.
  6. Restart anytime:
    Click the Easy Worship logo at the top‑left or press the Restart button.
